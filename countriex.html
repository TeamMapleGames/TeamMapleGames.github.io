CountrieX - cross-platform multiplayer sandbox game about controling a country as if it was real (almost, may be not accurate)!

In CountrieX, you can do anything!

Here's what you can do! (or game features):

  • Build your country from ground!
  • War other players! (DESTROY THEM!)
  • Make peace on this sinful earth (near impossible)
  • Create your own country/texture pack/sounds or something else! (You can mod this game whatever you want!)
